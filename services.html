Design

Recognizing the fact that usability, functionality and visualization are three of the most important factors when designing interfaces or web sites, I have developed a strategy along with working with you to ensure that your messaging and marketing goals are realized with the designs we create together.

I focus on gathering as much of your company's information through a pre-development survey before the design is started. By adhering to this practice we are able to develop a design concept that will meet both your expectations and your marketing goals.

Development

HTML and Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) are the backbone of all web design projects. It is of utmost importance that every site I develop be well organized, using the latest HTML and CSS standards along with incorporating JavaScript and other tools to give your site a clean and friendly responsive theme.

The goal is to ensure a friendly user experience, as well as lay the groundwork for optimal search engine exposure.

Web Management

Another service I offer my clientele is web site maintenance. Through this service, I am able to provide you with recommendations to reach your marketing goals, make simple text updates as well as add additional pages of content to help in your SEO efforts. This service is avaliable to all new and existing clients even if your website was developed by another party.

Unlike other online managment services, you will be able to comunicate directly with me about updating your site. No run arounds here.

Web Hosting

I provide a basic hosting solution for your new or existing web site that is not only cost effective but delivers performance. This is focused towards HTML/CSS, Blogs, RSS Feeds and more. The best part about this serivice is that your website is hosted and managed locally but also completely owned by YOU. No need to go through all the red tape online services are known to create.
